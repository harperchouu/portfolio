While observing how much effort fans devote to following their idols, we wanted to better understand the psychology and behavior behind fandom culture. We interviewed nearly 20 fans, as well as a counseling psychologist and professors in related fields, to analyze the consumer strategies of entertainment companies and explore how fans perceive their dedication.

By combining the fan culture report and the ticket scalping investigation, I designed a simulated ticketing system to help users experience the stress and urgency of competing for tickets.
